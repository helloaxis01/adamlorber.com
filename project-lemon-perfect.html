Lemon Perfect

Freelance art direction and copywriting for Lemon Perfect, a Beyoncé-backed zero sugar organic beverage looking to make a name for itself. This project specifically was ambitious and aimed at getting their name and brand out there, with a billboard campaign built to challenge Coca-Cola in their own backyard while being memorable to anyone driving by.

RoleFreelance Art Director
ScopeOOH campaign, copywriting, retail
ClientLemon Perfect
MarketAtlanta
01 · Challenge

Lemon Perfect had a clear value proposition in zero sugar and organic ingredients, but needed a stronger personality to stand out in a category still dominated by legacy beverage brands.

02 · Approach

I led art direction and copywriting for the Atlanta campaign, pairing minimal visuals with confident headlines built for both a highway glance and the grocery aisle.

01 / 03

Original OOH concept: minimalist visuals paired with punchy headlines like "Lemon + water. A real classic."

01 / 03

A second direction split the board: product and tagline on one side, the brand’s signature yellow cap owning the other.

03 · Outcome

I delivered a full OOH and in store concept package, including board directions, headlines, and dump bin design for Lemon Perfect’s home market.
