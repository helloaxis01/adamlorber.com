Case study · Client Work

Dream Clean

A full label redesign that moved a woman/Black-owned cleaning brand from local shelves toward national competitors like Mrs. Meyer's.

RoleArt Director
ScopeLabel design, copywriting, icons
OwnershipWoman & Black-owned
PositioningPlant-based, cruelty-free
01 · Challenge

Dream Clean's original packaging didn't communicate how well the product worked. It needed to compete visually with national household names.

01 / 03

Before: the original label didn't signal quality or communicate how well the product actually worked.

02 · Approach

I designed a premium label system with custom icons translating plant-based, cruelty-free benefits at a glance, anchored by the line “Cleaning products with purpose.”

01 / 03

After: a premium look built to signal quality the moment a shopper walks down the aisle.

01 / 03

Side by side, the full extent of the redesign: from generic label to a shelf-ready system.

03 · Outcome

The rebrand won shelf space at Lazy Acres with active expansion plans into Sprouts and Whole Foods, and the company expanded into a full cleaning service using its own products.

The finished packaging system across the full scent line: Lavender, Lime Thyme, and MintTea.
