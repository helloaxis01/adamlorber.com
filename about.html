About

I'm an Art Director and Lead Designer based in Los Angeles. For sixteen years I directed visual identity for the Television Academy and the Emmy Awards, the kind of work where the deadline is a live event and there's no version two.

Since then I've taken that discipline into health tech, building brand and product for startups like Fount and Inflection, and into independent client work across CPG, hospitality, and brand identity.

I'm currently consulting for the Television Academy on the 2026 Emmy season and serving as Art Director III (contract) for Amazon Prime Video Sports, while taking on select client projects.

When I'm not working, you can find me going on adventures with my wife and son, taking our dog on a long walk, in the kitchen, snapping photos, and exploring new places.
