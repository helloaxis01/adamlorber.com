PUBLIC PRETTY URLS
------------------
Netlify `_redirects` maps clean paths to the HTML files, e.g.:

  /fount                 → project-fount.html
  /television-academy    → project-television-academy.html
  /amazon                → project-amazon-prime-video-sports.html
  /process               → studies-process.html
  /about                 → about.html

Hitting the .html filename redirects to the pretty path.
Legacy /televisionacademy redirects to /television-academy.
