01 · Challenge

Fount needed to transition from a startup concept to a wellness powerhouse. As the lead designer, I was tasked with building a cohesive visual system from the ground up while simultaneously launching their first consumer-facing product, FlyKitt, under a very tight timeline.

I acted as the sole creative lead, working directly with the leadership team. I was responsible for everything from initial brand strategy and packaging design to art directing photoshoots and collaborating with engineers on the mobile app interface.

To keep the brand identity consistent across websites, packaging, and digital collateral, I developed comprehensive brand guidelines, so the experience felt unified whether a member was holding a product box or using the app. Shots below from the product photography shoot featuring the final FlyKitt packaging design.

When photo shoot costs were quickly creeping up, I proposed an alternative using AI to deliver the same high-end result without the production costs. Using Midjourney and Photoshop, I created a realistic hero image that became the face of the FlyKitt product for over three years, then built an entire library of AI-generated assets integrated throughout the app and website.

The face of FlyKitt, an AI image created with Midjourney, ChatGPT, and generative AI in Photoshop, used in place of an on-location photo shoot and saving the company upwards of $50K.

For the FlyKitt app and website, I worked closely with engineering to simplify the interface, making complex performance data intuitive at a glance. Since many users interact with the app during night flights, a permanent dark mode was implemented to reduce eye strain. That work helped the team reach a 4.7-star rating in the App Store.

FlyKitt app icon.

03 · Outcome

The brand identity I established helped position Fount as a premium player in the wellness space. Successfully launched FlyKitt in 30 days, designed investor decks fundamental in securing the company's Series A funding, and created a suite of assets, from FDA-compliant packaging to brand videos, that maintained high visual standards as the company scaled.

Fount announces $12M Series A. Business Wire, March 2023.
