Concept

Screens, desks, and stress contribute to back pain and tech neck. AXIS is the reset button — guided movement that dials down pain and wakes up your core.

Movement equals medicine. Not a workout. A nervous system reset with proven moves, quick resets or deeper sessions, and cues so you can stay in the flow.

I’m designing and building it end-to-end: brand system, onboarding, session UX, iconography that shifts with time of day, and native shells for iOS and Android.
