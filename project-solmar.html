Solmar

Naming, identity, and packaging for a premium Mediterranean sea jerky lifestyle brand.

RoleArt Director
ScopeNaming, identity, packaging
CategoryCPG / premium snack
PositioningMediterranean lifestyle
01 · Challenge

Solmar needed to launch a premium sea jerky that felt like a lifestyle brand rather than another shelf-stable snack. The challenge was to move away from the gritty aesthetic of traditional jerky and capture the essence of Mediterranean coastal living.

02 · Approach

I led brand development from the ground up, including helping name the product, the full visual identity, and the packaging system, so sustainability and nutrient-dense ingredients felt built into the brand.

Inspiration came from Italian coastal towns: typography and design that feel laid-back yet chic. Bold, colorful stripes reminiscent of iconic Italian beach umbrellas help the packaging stop shoppers in the aisle.

Color and custom iconography anchor the brand in sun-cured methods and ocean purity, so sustainability reads as part of the personality rather than a marketing claim.

01 / 10
03 · Outcome

A fully developed, shelf-ready brand system that positions Solmar as a premium lifestyle snack, balancing a nutrient-dense offer (including 7 grams of protein and high omega-3 content) with a fresh visual voice for design-conscious health consumers.
