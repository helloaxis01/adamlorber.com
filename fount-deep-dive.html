In late September 2022, I joined Fount, a wellness and tech startup dedicated to optimizing health and performance for the world's top performers. As Lead Designer and the sole creative on the team, I had the opportunity to shape the brand from the ground up, driving design for a game-changing business.

Key achievements

  • Comprehensive Rebrand & Product LaunchesI led a full-scale rebranding of Fount and launched its first public product, FlyKitt, a jet lag solution, in my first 30 days with the company. This included overseeing the design of Squarespace websites for both brands and delivering on a tight November 1st deadline.
  • Decks & Strategic MarketingDesigned decks, sales one-pagers, and promotional materials to support company growth. This included presentations for high-profile celebrity collaborations and partnerships, as well as investor decks that played a key role in securing Fount's successful Series A funding round.
  • Packaging DesignDesigned FDA-compliant, consumer-friendly packaging for FlyKitt and the Fount Kitt System wellness line, launched in November 2023. Additionally, I designed a custom welcome box to house Fount and FlyKitt products for new members.
  • Brand DevelopmentCreated essential brand assets, including internal communications collateral, membership materials, and comprehensive brand guidelines, ensuring a cohesive identity across all platforms.
  • Photo & Video ProductionProduced and art directed a photo and video shoot, ultimately creating a comprehensive catalog of lifestyle and product photos for Fount and FlyKitt. In tandem, I oversaw video production, which was later utilized in a brand video, a testimonial video, and two 20-second hero videos featured on the Fount and FlyKitt websites.
  • Creative Content ProductionConceptualized and coordinated additional brand videos, ensuring alignment with Fount's evolving identity and messaging.
  • Digital Design & Web DevelopmentSpearheaded the WordPress website redesign for Fount and FlyKitt, collaborating with developers to enhance UI/UX and meet aggressive timelines.
  • Mobile App UI/UXPartnered with the CTO and engineering team to design the user interface for Fount and FlyKitt mobile apps, contributing icons, graphics, and UI/UX improvements.
  • AI & Innovative Cost-Saving SolutionsLeveraged AI tools (Midjourney, ChatGPT, Adobe Firefly, Photoshop Generative AI) to create marketing visuals, saving the company over $50,000 in production costs.

While much of my work remains confidential, the projects highlighted here showcase my ability to lead design initiatives across branding, product, digital, and marketing channels, all within a fast-paced, high-growth environment.

I designed the FlyKitt App Store screens and contributed to the app's UI. Considering that many FlyKitt users will be interacting with the app during night flights, a permanent dark mode was implemented to reduce eye strain. Bright colors and simple icons were also used for easy visibility in dark environments.

I also designed the FlyKitt app icon, which represents a flight path from departure to cruising altitude with a simple grey line. The line reflects FlyKitt's functionality in aiding the body to transition from a sluggish state to being functional, ultimately helping the user avoid jet lag. Additionally, the icon features a four-quadrant circle in the bottom right corner, the Fount mark, signifying it being powered by Fount AI.
